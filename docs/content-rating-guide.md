## Additional Information

### Medical Content Disclaimer
The app contains medical information and references to medications, but:
- All content is for legitimate medical purposes
- No recreational drug references
- No graphic medical content
- No disturbing medical imagery
- All content is educational and health-focused

### Privacy Considerations
- No collection of sensitive personal health data beyond basic medication tracking
- No sharing of medical information with third parties
- User has full control over their data
- Clear privacy policy and account deletion options

### Target Audience
- Adults managing their own medications
- Caregivers managing medications for others
- Healthcare professionals looking for patient management tools
- Anyone 13+ who needs medication reminders

## Recommended Content Rating: **Everyone (3+)**

### Justification:
- No violence, nudity, or inappropriate content
- Educational and health-focused content
- Simple, clean interface suitable for all ages
- Medical content is purely informational and non-graphic
- No harmful or disturbing content

## Age Appropriateness
- **Minimum Age**: 13+
- **Target Demographic**: Adults 18-65+
- **Suitable for**: All ages (with parental guidance for minors)

## Regional Considerations
- **US**: ESRB - Everyone
- **Europe**: PEGI - 3
- **Global**: IARC - Everyone

## Notes for Review Process
- Emphasize the health and wellness nature of the app
- Highlight that medication references are for legitimate medical purposes only
- Point out the educational and supportive nature of the content
- Mention the clean, professional interface design
- Note the absence of any harmful or inappropriate content
